Shipping modules allow you to charge your customers for delivery. 

As of Zen Cart 1.5.6, the built in shipping modules are: 

- flat 
- freeoptions 
- freeshipper 
- item
- perweightunit
- storepickup
- table
- zones  

For more information on shipping modules, see [these pages](/user/shipping/).
